<h3 class="text-center"> 
Introduction
</h3>
<br>

It's a thrilling puzzle game where you play as Lila, a character who wakes up to find her consciousness trapped in a computer. With the help of a mysterious root dialog box, Lila must unravel the conspiracies surrounding digital immortality and fight for her freedom in the virtual world. With a mix of puzzle-solving and action elements, this game will keep you on the edge of your seat as you explore the unknown and make choices that determine Lila's fate.

<h3 class="text-center"> 
How to Play
</h3>
<br>
 
+ Press <font color=Crimson><b><i>A</i></b></font> and <font color=Crimson><b><i>D</i></b></font> to move around, and <font color=Crimson><b><i>Space</i></b></font> to jump.
+ Press <font color=Crimson><b><i>R</i></b></font> to restart the level. 
+ Press <font color=Crimson><b><i>return</i></b></font> to confirm. 
+ Press <font color=Crimson><b><i>ESC</i></b></font> to quit the game.
